# Dragon Dice: The Ultimate RPG Companion
Step into the tavern, pull up a chair, and let the fate of your next campaign rest in your hands. Whether you are striking down a red dragon, picking a masterfully crafted lock, or rolling for initiative in a crowded dungeon, Dragon Dice delivers the raw grit and thrill of tabletop gaming straight to your device.

Leave the plastic behind, or back up your favorite set, with a digital dice roller designed by adventurers, for adventurers.

## FEATURES TO FUEL YOUR ADVENTURE:

* The Full Arsenal: Instantly roll any standard polyhedral die required for your favorite RPG systems, including D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, and the legendary D20.
* Horde Rolling Made Easy: Need to cast a high-level Fireball? Scale up your dice pool seamlessly to roll multiple dice at the exact same time.
* Calculate with Precision: Easily add or subtract custom stat modifiers to your pool. Your final total is calculated instantly, complete with a detailed breakdown so you can track exactly how your dice landed.
* Immersive Fantasy Aesthetic: Game in style with a beautifully crafted, thematic interface that feels right at home next to your character sheets and miniatures.
* Fate in a Shake: Want that authentic, physical feel? Turn on the Shake to Roll feature in your settings to cast your dice with a flick of your wrist.
* Never Lose Focus: Don't let a sleeping screen ruin a clutch moment. Toggle the Stay Awake setting to keep your digital dice tray active and ready throughout the entire gaming session.

## HOW TO PLAY:

* Tap your chosen polyhedral die.
* Set your dice count and add your character's modifiers.
* Smash the ROLL button (or if enabled in settings, shake your device) and let the fates decide your destiny!
* Click the result panel to see individual die rolls instead of the summary. Click again to go back.
* Your next great adventure is just a roll away. Download Dragon Dice today and let the critical hits commence!
