# Privacy Policy for Dragon Dice

**Last Updated: May 17, 2026**

Your privacy is incredibly important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how **Dragon Dice** handles information when you use our iOS mobile application. 

The short answer is: **We don't collect, store, or share your personal data.**

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use
Dragon Dice is built with a "privacy by design" philosophy. 
* **No Personal Data:** We do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location).
* **No Account Creation:** You do not need to create an account or log in to use any features of the app.
* **No Tracking:** We do not use any third-party tracking software, analytics frameworks, or advertising networks that monitor your behavior across the web or other apps.

## 2. On-Device Storage
Any data generated within the app—such as your custom character settings, dice preferences, or roll history—is stored **locally and exclusively on your own iOS device**. 
* This data never leaves your device.
* We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any external servers.
* If you delete the app, this locally stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services
Besides a link to our X.com page, Dragon Dice does not include links to external websites, third-party frameworks, or SDKs that collect data, outside of standard Apple App Store services (such as anonymous crash reports provided directly by Apple if you have opted-in to share them with developers).

## 4. Children's Privacy
Because our application does not collect any personal information from any users, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect future app updates or changes in legal requirements. Any updates will be posted directly to this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to us through our official support channel:

* [**Dragon Dice Support Page**](https://AnnawanDev.github.io/Support)
